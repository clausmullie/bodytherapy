What is body therapy?

Body therapy allows you to work with the physical body to learn more about the inner self.

Each experience leaves a trace, remembered by the body; conditioning the mind. Unblocking physical tensions in the body helps integrate unprocessed emotions, which in turn helps getting 'unstuck' from negative thought loops and subconscious behaviour patterns.

The aim is to feel more present, at home in your own body, and more aware in your relationship with yourself and the world around you.

Individual Sessions

Individual sessions combine massage techniques with breathwork, pressure points, and reflexology.

I will use touch to surface old psycho-emotional tensions - whether stress, anxiety, fear, anger, sadness - as well as the restrictive thoughts, feelings and behaviour patterns that come with them. Once surfaced, you have the opportunity to work with your breathing, mind and nervous system to let go of these tensions.

As these tensions are released, you may feel a relaxed presence in your body, clarity of mind, a deep sense of relief, and the ability to more fully enjoy life and navigate its changes.

Group Trainings

Trainings combine elements from reflexology, traditional karate and yoga to open up the body and mind.

There is a strong emphasis on grounding and becoming more aware of how you handle limits, escape or face internal resistance, and can move from a deeper presence.

They also help to release joints and muscles, stimulate circulation, promote body awareness and balance, and strengthen the body.

About me
