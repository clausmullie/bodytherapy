Why body therapy?

Each experience leaves a trace, remembered by the body; conditioning the mind.

Body therapy helps you release old phyco-emotional tensions, let go of old restrictive beliefs and mental patterns, and allow you to feel more peaceful, relaxed and clear.

Individual Treatments

ManuVision treatments combine massage with breathwork, pressure points and reflexology to give you the opportunity to let go of old emotions - fear, insecurity, anger, sadness, grief,...

The emphasis lies on recognizing the emotional baggage you carry, how it affects your daily life, nervous system and over or under reactions to triggers

Group Trainings

ManuVision training combines reflexology with traditional karate and yoga to open up the body and mind by releasing joints and muscles, stimulating circulation, and promoting body awareness and balance.

The emphasis lies becoming aware of how we handle limits, how we escape or face internal resistance, and how we can move from a deeper presence.

Community events

Community events include a yearly 4-day fasting retreat, as well as a reading group and meditation circle. Sign up to the mailing list to find out more!

About

I am a therapist-in-training, have been working within ManuVision since 2016 (first as client and now as a student), and have been dabbling with the existential arts for over 15 years. My other mask is helping governments with open source software.

ManuVision is an international community of body therapists using tools to work with the physical body as a means to learn more about the inner self. Find out more at manuvision.es/en
