The Body Sessions

Body-based therapy to help you untangle yourself.
Let go of what you are not to discover what you are.









Open body, open mind

Each experience leaves a trace, remembered by the body, conditioning the mind.

Body-based therapy helps you release old physical tensions, integrate unprocessed emotions & experiences, and free your nervous system from unhelpful subconscious reflexes.

Unblocking your body, you unblock your mind - untangling yourself from worn-out narratives, sticky thought loops, and toxic behaviour patterns.

Individual sessions

During individual sessions I use massage techniques and pressure points to surface subconscious psycho-emotional tensions in your body - such as old stress, anxiety, fear, anger, sadness and unprocessed emotions or experiences.

Once surfaced, you have the opportunity to work with your breathing, mind and nervous system to let go of these tensions, as well as the restrictive thoughts, feelings and behaviour patterns associated with them.

Sessions will help you feel relaxed and present in your body, with a clearer mind, and more able to fully enjoy life and navigate change. Or feel a sudden desire to sign up to a pottery course and start wearing purple. ¯\_(ツ)_/¯

Group sessions

Group sessions combine elements from reflexology, traditional karate, and yoga to open up the body and mind. There is a strong emphasis on grounding and becoming more aware of how you handle limits, escape or face internal resistance, and can move from a deeper presence.

About

My name is Claus and I have been dabbling with the existential arts for the past 15 years, with a particular fondness for lucid dreaming, meditation, body work and shadow work.

I am currently training to be a Manuvision therapist. Manuvision is an international community of body therapists specialised in working with the nervous system. I have been working with Manuvision since 2016 - first as a client and now as a student.

Read more about Manuvision Body Therapy, Manuvision Treatments, Manuvision Trainings.

Community sessions

Community sessions include a poetry reading group, a meditation circle, sporadic lucid dreaming workshops, and a yearly 4-day fasting retreat. More information coming soon.
